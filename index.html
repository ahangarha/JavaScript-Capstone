About

Taste Jamaica is a collection of Jamaican meals. You may like them and leave comment for each. We chose Jamaican meals as an excuse for ourselves to know more about a lesser known culture.

This website is made as an educational project. Here we examined our skills for calling external APIs to fetch and submit data.
